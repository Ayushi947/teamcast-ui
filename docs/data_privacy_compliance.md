# Data Privacy Compliance for AI Interviewer & Recruiter Platforms

This document outlines key privacy obligations under **GDPR (EU)**, **CCPA (California, USA)**, and **DPDP (India)** that apply to AI-powered hiring platforms, including video interviewers, automated screeners, and candidate scoring systems.

---

## 1. GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation – EU)

- **Consent**: Obtain explicit consent before collecting or processing personal data (e.g., resumes, interview recordings, assessments).
- **Data Minimization**: Collect only the data necessary for recruitment-related activities.
- **Right to Access / Erasure**: Candidates can request access to their personal data and have the right to have it deleted.
- **Automated Decision-Making**:
  - Inform candidates when AI is used in decision-making.
  - Provide an option for human review upon request.
  - Ensure AI decisions are explainable (transparency of criteria).
- **Cross-Border Transfers**:
  - Ensure legal safeguards are in place when transferring candidate data outside the EU (e.g., SCCs).

---

## 2. CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

- **Notice at Collection**: Clearly inform users at the time of data collection about what is being collected and why.
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Allow users to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information.
- **Right to Access and Delete**: Provide tools for users to view and delete their data.
- **Non-Discrimination**: Do not penalize users who exercise their privacy rights.
- **AI Use Disclosure**: Be transparent about the use of AI in recruiting workflows.

---

## 3. DPDP (Digital Personal Data Protection Act – India, 2023)

- **Consent-Based Processing**: Collect and process personal data only after obtaining consent from the user.
- **Purpose Limitation**: Use the collected data strictly for recruitment purposes only.
- **Data Principal Rights**:
  - Right to access, correction, erasure, and grievance redressal.
- **Data Fiduciary Responsibilities**:
  - Ensure data accuracy, implement security safeguards, and set up grievance mechanisms.
- **Cross-Border Data Flow**:
  - Allowed by default unless restricted by government notification.

---

## Implementation Guidelines for AI Hiring Platforms

- ✅ Present a **clear privacy notice** during user onboarding.
- ✅ Implement **consent mechanisms** for interviews, data collection, and AI usage.
- ✅ Provide **access controls** and **data management dashboards** for users.
- ✅ Maintain **human oversight** for automated screening or ranking decisions.
- ✅ Store data securely and audit data processing activities regularly.
- ✅ Tailor privacy policies and UX/UI flow for users based on their **geographical jurisdiction**.
